Global Cognition Research Corridor

The Global Cognition Research Corridor is a basic science research and technology transition program in the neurosciences, Artificial Intelligence and VR/AR and 360 degree vision. Recent progress in the neurosciences has greatly advanced our knowledge of how brain function underlies behavior, providing the modern foundations for understanding how we sense, perceive, and interact with the world. These understandings, if properly leveraged, have the potential to provide revolutionary advances, fostering practical solutions to address Army needs.

Launched in July 2018, the GCRC brings together world-class researchers, experienced industry partners, and some of the World’s brightest scientists to harness the vast, worldwide investment in neuroscience Cognition research and development. The GCRC’s program of scientific research and development is aimed at advancing and accelerating the maturation of neuroscience-based approaches with Artificial Intelligence implementations understanding Soldier performance in operational environments and to enhancing future Soldier-system development.
